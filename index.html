Supporting a Student's Mental Health

Facts and Figures

Why is student mental health important?

  • 24% of HCPSS high school students reported feeling sad or hopeless in the last year
  • 18% of HCPSS middle school students reported feeling sad or hopeless in the last year
  • 1 in 9 youth in Howard County diagnosed with either depression and anxiety

*Howard County School Health Council- Howard Co Health Assessment Survey and 2014 Maryland Youth Risk Behavior Survey
